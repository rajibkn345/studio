# **App Name**: RTG Navigator

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Display a hero section with headline 'Investing in Great Businesses. Elevating Them with Technology and Experience', a subheadline '100+ years of operational excellence meets innovation,' CTA buttons for 'Sell Your Business', 'Franchise With Us', and 'Partner With RTG,' and a video loop or hero image montage showcasing hospitality and tech.
- Trust Bar: Display logos of partner/portfolio brands and companies the team has worked with.
- About Preview: Include a short blurb and a 'Learn More' link to the About RTG page.
- Portfolio Highlights: Showcase a carousel of 3-4 recent investments with mini case stats.
- Franchise Spotlight: Include an image and callout with an 'Explore Franchise Opportunities' button.
- Sell Your Business CTA Banner: Display a banner with the message 'Ready to exit? Let's protect your legacy' and a contact button.
- Leadership Preview: Feature a team image, quote, and link to the Team page.
- Inquiry Form: Enable direct inquiries through a well-placed, intuitive contact form.
- Team Introduction: Use generative AI tool to create short engaging bio blurbs that reflects people-first leadership.
- Footer: Include quick links, an email signup, social links (LinkedIn, Instagram), and legal/privacy links.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Navy blue (#1A237E) to convey trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Light gray (#ECEFF1) for a clean and modern aesthetic.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) to highlight key elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif.
- Use elegant, professional icons that align with the luxury tone.
- Employ a clean, concise layout with ample whitespace to ensure readability and a refined feel.
- Incorporate subtle motion effects, such as smooth scrolling and fade-in animations, to enhance user experience without distraction.
